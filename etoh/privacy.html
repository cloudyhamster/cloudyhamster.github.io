Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

When you log in to EToH Progression via Roblox Open Cloud, we only access and store your public Roblox User ID, Username, Display Name, and Avatar URL.

We do not access your password, Robux balance, game inventory, or friends list.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used to provide, maintain, and improve the EToH Progression experience. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Authenticating your session to ensure secure access to your profile.
  • Personalizing your experience by saving preferences, progress, favorites, and other user-specific settings.
  • Displaying your statistics on public leaderboards or activity feeds if you choose to participate.
  • Analyzing aggregated data to improve site performance and features.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Data Deletion (Right to Erasure)

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your data at any time. To exercise this right:

  1. Join our Discord Server.
  2. Open a ticket or contact a moderator.
  3. Provide your Roblox Username and request a "Data Wipe."

We will permanently remove all database records associated with your User ID within 30 days of your request.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to aggregate anonymous usage statistics (e.g., most viewed towers, user regions). This data is vital for the development of this portfolio project.
